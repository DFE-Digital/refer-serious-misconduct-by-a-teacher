What happens next

Your referral will be reviewed within 3 working days.

It can take up to 20 weeks from the date of your referral for TRA to make a decision whether to investigate your case.

If a decision is not made within 14 days of receiving your referral, you’ll be sent an email to let you know.

TRA could decide that they:

  • need more information
  • will not investigate the case, for example because it’s unlikely to result in a prohibition order
  • will launch a formal investigation
