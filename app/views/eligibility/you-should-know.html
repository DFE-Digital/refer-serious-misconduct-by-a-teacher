If your allegation of serious misconduct is investigated, it could result in the person you’re referring being banned from teaching.

You might need to attend a hearing of a professional conduct panel to give evidence, if the allegation reaches that stage.

Your name and the allegation will be shared with the person you’re referring, and any employer. Your contact details will not be shared.
