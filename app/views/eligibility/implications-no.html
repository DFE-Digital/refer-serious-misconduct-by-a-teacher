Follow these steps to make a complaint:

1. Complain to the school

Every school in England must have a complaints policy.

It’s usually available on the school’s website or from the school office. It should tell you the kind of complaints the school deals with.

Find out how to complain to the school.

2. If you’re not satisfied with how the school deals with your complaint

{#

Complain to DfE

#}

You can make a complaint to the Department for Education (DfE) if either of the following apply:

  • you have completed the school’s complaints process
  • you have been stopped from completing the school’s complaints process
