Who you can report

You can only report a teacher for serious misconduct if their day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • planning and preparing lessons for pupils
  • giving lessons to pupils
  • assessing and reporting on the development and progress of pupils

You can report a teacher who has resigned or been dismissed.

Who you cannot report

The TRA will not usually consider cases relating to:

  • teaching assistants
  • head teachers
  • school support staff
