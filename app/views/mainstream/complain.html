Complain to the school, college or council

Before you report a teacher, you should complain to:

  • the school or sixth form college
  • the board of governors
  • your local council

Depending on the case, the TRA might not investigate unless you've completed the school's complaints procedure.

If you do not think your complaint was handled correctly, you can complain to the Department for Education.
