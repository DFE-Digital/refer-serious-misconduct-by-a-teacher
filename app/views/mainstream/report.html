Report serious teacher misconduct online

{{ govukWarningText({ html: 'Contact the police if you think a child is in immediate danger.' }) }}

Use this service to report serious teacher misconduct in England if you're:

  • a member of the public
  • an employer

Before you start

You’ll need to provide:

  • your contact details and information about the teacher you are reporting
  • evidence to support your allegation

If your case is investigated:

  • your name and allegation will be shared with the teacher and their employer
  • you might need to give evidence at a hearing
  • your referral will be kept on file for up to 50 years

Once you've signed in, you'll be able to save your answers and come back later to complete the referral.
