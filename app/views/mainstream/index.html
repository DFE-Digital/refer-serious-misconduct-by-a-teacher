Overview

The Teaching Regulation Agency (TRA) is responsible for regulating the teaching profession in England.

In cases of serious teacher misconduct where you have already been through the school's complaints procedure, you can report the teacher to the TRA.

The TRA will only consider cases if the misconduct is serious enough to potentially result in the teacher being prevented from teaching again.
