Examples of serious misconduct are:

  • violent behaviour
  • a serious criminal offence
  • an abuse of position or trust
  • inappropriate sexual activity
  • a serious failure in safeguarding
  • a sexual or romantic relationship with a current or former pupil

If your allegation is not serious misconduct, it will not be investigated. For example, cases of incompetence.
