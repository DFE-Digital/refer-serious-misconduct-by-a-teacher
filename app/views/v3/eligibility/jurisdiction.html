You can only refer a teacher for serious misconduct if their day-to-day responsibilities include:

  • planning and preparing lessons for pupils
  • giving lessons to pupils
  • assessing and reporting on the development and progress of pupils

You can refer a teacher who has resigned or been dismissed.

Who you cannot refer

The TRA will not usually consider cases relating to:

  • teaching assistants
  • head teachers
  • school support staff

You should complain to the school instead.
