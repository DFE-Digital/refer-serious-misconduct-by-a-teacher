What we mean by 'teacher'

For the purposes of teacher regulation, the TRA defines a teacher as someone who carries out teaching work in a classroom or online such as:

  • planning and preparing lessons for pupils
  • giving lessons to pupils
  • assessing and reporting on the development and progress of pupils

This does not include teaching assistants or other support staff who work under the supervision of teachers.
