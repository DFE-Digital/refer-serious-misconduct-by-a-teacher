You can only refer a teacher if their responsibilities include:

  • planning, preparing and delivering lessons to pupils
  • assessing and reporting on the development and progress of pupils

You can refer a teacher:

  • who has resigned or been dismissed
  • for serious misconduct outside of the school

Who you cannot refer

The TRA will not usually consider cases relating to:

  • teaching assistants, except if their responsibilities include those of a teacher listed above
  • school support staff, for example caretakers or administrators

You should complain to the school instead.
