Every school in England must have a complaints policy. It’s usually available on the school’s website or from the school office.

You may also be able to complain to your local council or organisations such as the school inspectors Ofsted. This depends on the type of school and what you’re complaining about.

Find out how to complain to the school or another organisation.

Complain to the Department for Education (DfE)

You can make a complaint to the DfE about a school if any of the following apply:

  • you’ve completed the school’s complaints process
  • you’ve been stopped from completing the school’s complaints process
  • a child is not getting an education
  • a child has been exposed to harm
